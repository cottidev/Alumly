# 🌌 Alumly
**Plan. Think. Learn.**

Alumly is a high-performance, minimalist study hub designed for deep focus. It combines a dynamic spatial canvas for brainstorming with a structured resource management system to help you bridge the gap between messy thoughts and polished knowledge.

---

## ✨ Core Pillars

* **Spatial Canvas:** A distraction-free environment to map out ideas, connect concepts, and visualize your learning journey.
* **Resource Hub:** A centralized home for your notes, external research links (ChatGPT, Gemini, etc.), and media.
* **Immersive Editor:** A professional, "Word-style" writing experience optimized for long-form synthesis and PDF exporting.
* **Global Sync:** Seamless cloud persistence powered by Firebase, ensuring your workspace is available wherever you are.
* **Minimalist Design:** A sleek, "Slate & Charcoal" aesthetic that stays out of your way so you can focus on what matters.

---

## 🚀 Quick Start

1.  **Launch:** Open the dashboard to view your active subjects.
2.  **Create:** Add a new subject and choose an icon that represents your goal.
3.  **Ideate:** Use the canvas to drop notes and create connections between ideas.
4.  **Refine:** Open the **Resources** tab to transform those messy thoughts into a structured document or PDF.
5.  **Finish:** Mark subjects as completed, and keep your dashboard clean.
